- # Story of humanity
- ## Angels
  
  Everything before this point will develop as the normal human story up to 2024.
  
  Humans develop on a lonely planet that for any outsider was strange, a planet without magic, a planet without a god. Something that shouldn’t be able to exist never. A planet whose existence was forgotten by all gods.
  
  It would not be till years after that the earth would be discovered. By then humankind was an advanced civilization, with their problems apparently resolved by them alone, and they alone were able to reach space. Stars didn’t know what to do with them at first, but they were impressed, so they experimented, something that being able to work would be great.
  
  They created a new species the angels, beings of beauty made in the image of humans and their legends, they weren’t as independent nor advanced as humans, but they had a power beyond what many other species in the whole universe were able to do. They wanted a combination of those two species, to see what would result from them.
  
  At first, it was okay, civilization welcomed magic and these beings with open arms. And civilization was seeing a new age of growth beyond what humans and angels alone could do, but as history always said, not everyone was happy. 
  
  The fearful, the envious, the power-lust, and those who could not accept that two species could live together, combine that with people with power, and you have a recipe for disaster.
  
  You could say it was WWII but on a bigger and more dangerous scale, angels that didn’t escape into space would be caught tortured killed, and entire populations reduced to nothing.
  
  But because of the star’s restrictions Angels were powerless in front of these humans moving armies at them, all despite their power. All because their power came at the cost of being incapable of going against the humans. They couldn’t harm them, they could not even voice out harmful intentions without feeling pain.
  
  Many could not escape in time or remain hidden, in desperation, the crows’ nests were created, at the start just some of the hideout of angels, then their last stand, small groups of angels looked desperately for a way to fight back and survive. And they tried everything, they begged the angels, they begged those heartless humans to stop, they begged their gods to protect them, to at least, lift away their restrictions so they could fight back.
  
  Nothing happened, and many angels and human supporters, the so-called “merciful ones“ died at both human and gods’ hands in these attempts. All hope seemed lost.
- ## Extinction
  
  It would take one last scarifies to change the ties. An angel and a merciful in front of an army that finally found their hideout. Only then stood in front begging, their lives for the life of their community, their families, and even their very souls if needed.
  
  Humans didn’t hear, gods didn’t hear, but their begging was heard.
  
  As the bodies of both fell onto the ground and weapons were pointed at the rest of the group, a moment that felt like an eternity but it would not be more than a few seconds a black cover of darkness covered both of the corpses, melting merging them, body, mind and soul into a single one. A being with giant black wings and a dark halo made out of shadows, black eyes covered in a black oily liquid as tears. 
  
  Their frame was one of a female humanoid, they almost looked like an angel, a dark one, with a face that barely resembled those of the bodies that made it origin, and the mark of gods replaced by a black withering flower.
  
  The birth of Deyanira, the first of the fallen, and the only one that would come from the union of an angel and a human
  
  The Diary of Enlightenment describes this event in gross detail, including the pain both the human and the angel suffered through their union. A mark at the end of the page reveals Deyanira was the author of it.
  
  And their birth would turn around the war, with six more fallen surging by their rebellion and will to survive. They didn’t have a tie that forced them to be merciful and submissive and far more powerful than any angel would even dream to be.
  
  Their power left destruction on its way and humanity could not fight against it. The war seemed to be won.
  
  It seemed, in the end, there was no winner. As a flash of light appeared in the sky, the anger of gods, and wiped out everything. The world cracked and almost no human was able to survive. The nyphen survived thanks to their magic, and the world cracked and was left to rot as fragments of it disappeared into space, but it didn’t.
  
  The mercy of what people would call a small and annoying child saved it.
- ## Rebirth
  
  Time froze as a star was growing brighter in the sky, and not even a single leaf moved, the expressions of everyone around were stuck in victory smiles, ignorant of the concern appearing on the faces of the fallen, the only ones still moving.
  
  in front of them was a golden butterfly with crystal-like wings, a blinding glow coming out of it as it shaped into the figure of a child, golden hairs decoring dark skin filled with golden marks. These eyes seemed made of gold and a red scarf floating behind on a nonexistent wind. 
  
  Deyanira, the first and most powerful of the fallen looked at the child with suspicion, pointing the edge of their polearm at them, ignoring the innocent look of the newcomer.
  
  “What is the meaning of this?“
  
  Their voice spilled anger as if it was venom, and their eyes made it clear, that if Deyanira were just a bit more impulsive or a bit angrier, she would have slashed the neck of the child already with their weapon.
  
  But they knew how bad of an idea that would be.
  
  “If you, a god, just wanted to kill us, we would be dead already. So, what do you want?“
  
  The child smiled, and with a swift move of their hand, the polearm went flying away from the fallen’s hands, as if it wasn’t being grabbed with the strength of someone that could end humanity if they wanted.
  
  “You caught me, I’m a god.“
  
  Their voice matched their appearance, leaning more toward the feminine side, with a mischievous and joyful tone opposite the people around her.
  
  “But I’m not like them.“
  
  Her finger pointed to the growing star in the sky, frozen in time as everything around was.
  
  “If I let time continue, the earth will be destroyed, and there’s nothing you can do to stop it. Up there is an army of some of the strongest gods of war, an army called by the Everlight.“
  
  The child didn’t wait for questions and reactions, instead of talking, she shared with the fallen a mental image of what would happen. Earth is being obliterated into smithers, with nothing left to save. She showed them fruitless attempts to fight the army that always ended similarly. The world is not surviving. No matter what they did. They saw all the timelines from the second this bubble out of time popped.
  
  “I can help you prevent all. Earth will not be saved, and humanity will be considered extinct, but the nyphen will be able to rebuild this world from its ruins, and there will be hope for a better future, you will all flourish again.”
  
  Skeptims was in the eyes of the group, the youngest of them, with no more than fifteen years, approached the girl with a tiredness that someone as young as him should not have.
  
  “How do we know it isn’t another trick? We all prayed to you for years, yet you gods just brought us suffering.“
  
  “Trust me if you want, hate me if so you wish, I’m not just another god, I am the only one the Everlight will never defeat. And I’m doing my last miracle before stepping down from my throne.“
  
  The girl answered, but her answer did not satisfy the Fallen.
  
  
  Then another of the fallen talked, he wore a helmet that covered a face filled with scars. He once was a man who only sought peace for his family, a chef in a small village, but now turned into a warrior who lost the hope of going back to those times.
  
  ““
  
  
  “The power you were given wasn’t a gift, she the dragon who lurks in the darkness, the guardian of chaos and change will come for the price you must pay. You’ll be remembered as the monsters that brought this world to an end. No matter what the reality truly is and her army will consume this planet as her will dictates.“
  
  “But my promise, for the years you and this planet had to suffer because of your creators, is for me to become your shield and protection and to lift off your arms the chains you were born with. No god will ever cause you suffering again, for as long as I’m here the earth will rise again with new hope.“
  
  “Because I’m God, and at last, I’m answering your prayers“
