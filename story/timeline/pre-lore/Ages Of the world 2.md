## Rebirth age (0 - 3000 AH)

from humanity’s end to 7 thousand years ago. After earth was destroyed very little survived the attack, technology regressed thousands of years, most crops and animals died and civilization was fragmented into the new continents of the broken planet which was undergoing a new ice age

Without humans and with the gods gone the nyphen didn’t have an order to follow. As civilization rebuilt, wars were constant, so was hunger for the lack of natural growing crops, monarchs rose and fell constantly and any knowledge or artifact of humanity became sought treasures and hopes to survive.

The needs led to experimentation and this also became the age where the first and more archaic forms of proper spells and magic tech were created, inventions created to generate and use energy and be able to grow crops despite the cold winter, none of them were advanced to levels of the past humanity but they guaranteed the survival of Nyphen til the planet was able to heal on its own.

- ## Bloom age (3000 AH - 4500 AH)
  
  The records left to this day don’t give much clarity about what exactly happened, but most describe a progressively growth in the temperatures of the planet alongside the sudden appearance of strange creatures roaming the planet, at the time most taken as rumors because of the impossible descriptions of the fauna said to be appearing. New strange and magical flora also made an appearance as temperatures stopped being freezing cold. herbs able to miraculously heal fatal diseases, those that seemed to have a will on their own and inexplicable phenomena all around the planet.
  
  But it wasn’t until the first sight of a phoenix flying above a field of flowers growing among the snow that records consider the start of a new age of prosperity. 
  
  This was the age where the gods made their first appearance on earth after 3 thousand years, 7 gods claiming to come as a sorry gift for the suffering nyphen had to overcome for millennia.
  
  The bloom age characterizes itself as an age of new discovery and the first time new races unrelated to the nyphen or the undead started appearing, being the creations of the 7 gods that claimed earth under their protection.
  
  But there was one thing that made the event more important, gods didn’t came to claim a throne on the planet, all races they created and the once **before were given the world to govern and develop on their own, they are still worshipped why most of the planet inhabitants and they are the origin of miracles and prayer heard, but they aren’t monarchs.
