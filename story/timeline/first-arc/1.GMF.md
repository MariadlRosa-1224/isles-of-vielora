# Golden moon festival (GMF)

GMF is a more chill event as is just the opening, every 100 years since the end of the frozen age all of the planet's 5 moons are full at the same time and the biggest of them, in the past the single moon of earth gets a golden color, hence the name of the festival.

The festival last a week and is ended with the prophesies rain, shooting stars falling to the ground whispering about what the next century has to bring.

This year is special

## important characters appearing in the event

- fire god, Ember and Starry, they host the event this time around
- the dwarf, he is introduced at the end of the festival although no much is implied, he is a merchant trading powerful and useful gear for what seems to be a very specific kind of scrap
