# The Dwarf, The Goblin and The Automata (DGA)
- tags:: Story/Timeline/First_Arc, Events
- DGA doesn't start immediately after [GMF]([[The golden moon festival]]), it's build in the course of the side events happening in between. the dwarf, goblin and automata will appear during or after the side events to trade scrap found during the events in exchange for gear or information. The gear more often that not will come handy during the event or for the next one, to give an incentive to find scrap pieces.
  
  The door to ask and join them is always open.
  
  It will not be till the lux core is collected that the main part of the event starts.
- ## characters
- Bartholomew (dwarf), goblin, automata, builders of the machine
- stargaze council, and the mayor of stargaze, important in the final part
- the artisan god, first appearance
- ## Objectives
  
  Intro to human story and the remains legacy, her first {redacted}, awakening of the [[masked]] race, lead into [Soul chase]([[6_Soul-chase]]).
- ## Story
- ### Prelude
- They are all remnants and one is an automata, a magic core.
- What if all start as they found an artifact destroyed, a weapon used in the war (genocide) between humans and angels, a cruel weapon, a fusion of greatly advanced technology and magic.
-
- Despite being almost the last main event of the first arc, its importance come as early as [[The golden moon festival]] . Bartholomew introduces a mechanic where you can give him or his family, "a very rare piece of metal" in exchange for powerful gear. On its own the scrap is worthless so hopefully this will incentivize players to just give it to him.
  
  He will appear after or during side events showcasing his progress. At the start, nobody believed that what he was creating was going to be worth it. But, soon enough, the stuff he was creating became so complex and was showing so much promise. People became interested, starting investing. More scientists joined the project. At this point, OC's are allowed to join too. If the Oc that joined is a remnant, it would become even more important.
-
- ### Main Part
-
- It all started with intellectual curiosity, a group of inventors wanting to uncover the past, restoring the, at the moment, unknown artifact, but as they buy and get more pieces inside events and innocent but valuable trades with any oc that finds them, it started to become more than that.
  
  As they rebuild the machine, memories start coming back from deep within their souls, memories of great cities, of engineering labs, of methods, formulas, pieces and machinery. And then it stops being curiosity, they need to complete the machine, they need to complete the puzzle, the fragments of memories, before they know the machine becomes their lives and when they figure out it is a weapon more advanced that anything they have seen, it is too late for any of them to care.
  
  They start changing in their personalities, with manners unlike any of their races, confusing the past and the present, talking about stuff that "never" happened.
  
  Remains oc’s seeing the weapon
  
  Unless a remnant oc starts participating in the completion of the machine (I'll leave that door open) the effect will not be that prominent.
  
  But they will definitely feel something, some feel excited for the machine, others feel a deep fear and horror, others sadness and grief, some uncontrollable anger. It will be different from person to person, and their reaction will be a shadow of who they used to be.
  
  if/when the machine is finished, they fully believe they are in the times of war, they all believe they are humans, engineering delivering their greatest invention for human victory.
  
  |Or so the dwarf and the goblin believe, the automata is just awakening, their magic core illuminating with the light of a soul they aren't supposed to have.
  
  *What 's happening?
  
  Where am I?
  
  Why am I not dead?
  
  The light, the pain, the army of gods... the woman with white hair-
  
  Where is everyone? Mom, dad...*
  
  The two personalities inside the automata go on a hireway.
  
  And if you remember, is the automaton the one holding the weapon.
