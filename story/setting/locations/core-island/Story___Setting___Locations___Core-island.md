- # Core island
-
- Core island, the lowest Island in the world closer to the core. Mana in this place is extremely heavy, enough to crush in the lowest parts of it. This place is extremely dangerous, putting the ruins to shame.
  
  A way to describe it is hell on earth, a volcano of lava and mana made an island, with constant explosions of mana left and right enough to blow up pretty much anyone, creating holes straight to core (death), flight is impossible under the weight and the place is populated by monsters that adapted to both the weight of mana and learn to consume it to empower them self. The lowest ranking of fauna here is mid B.
  
  This place is home of what’s probably the greatest mana batteries you can harvest, if you are able to contain it, minerals, flora and fauna are all highly mana energized and old relics are also relatively common here in the mostly virgin land.
  
  But arguably the greatest thing you can find here hides in the lowest parts of the island. A garden filled with beautiful flowers, golden crystal butterflies dancing all around and in the center of it a girl with golden hair and chocolate skin staring at the sky.
  
  A God who gave away her omniscience for the sake of an identity. A child afraid of an enemy long forgotten by the world.
  
  You may convince her to follow you home, lower her guard and open earth to the outside.
  
  After this event space traveling becomes possible, and opens the door for new gods to appear.
