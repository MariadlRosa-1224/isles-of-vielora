- One of the most important villains in this history is the leader of the cult of the Eye of Wisdom. She, a woman, a little bit old but not too old. She is incredible, powerful. By her hand, any fist she touches can be destroyed in a second.
  
  But this power is not an ability from her. It comes from her necklace, a beautiful, yet awful necklace that within itself contains a pocket dimension, a dimension that serves as a prison for the void, the lost half of the guardian of order, the dead and the nothingness. In the pocket dimension lies a tower eternally surrounded by light.
  
  Chains are all around the place and if any unlucky person finds itself inside this place, the chains will also try to follow them and trap them. And once that happens, only the lost guardian will be able to free them. 
  
  The guardian doesn't have a name but may call herself [Midnight]([[MidNight-(Void)]]) to make herself different from her original and her other half. She's patient, very patient and being held by golden nearly indestructible chains for thousands of years barely seems to bother her. She's bothered a lot but she chose to keep herself calm and she cares. 
  
  She cares a lot about the things she remembers but the world has seemed to forget. Her memories aren't all nor clear, but one thing certain, she wants to see her little sister.
