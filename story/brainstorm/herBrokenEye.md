# Her broken eye

"I left you alone as you asked. what right did you think yu had
to come and touch what's mine"

The Everchanging Darkness screamed once more in anger, their claws pressing deep

into the shoulders of the Everlight. The sheer force was enough to shatter the ground under her body being anyone else her arms would have been destroyed by now, turned into a mush of meat and bone.

But for the Everlight it was barely enough force to hold her tight in place.

"My side has continued existing even after you decided to leave and never come back, after you decided to hunt down and slaughter my subjects and chosen ones."

Louder grew the Everchanging's screams, louder grew so their anger.

"Ten thousands years ago, you went to far, but I decided to trust in your judgement. "You wouldn't do something so drastic without a reason" I told myself."

"But then you threw away everything you were, everything you're meant, for what? A mask of purity, eternal existence and beauty? peace? You aren't any of that."

"Order is no simply some pretty shapes and tidy structures. You are the tide where growth doesn't become overgrowth. The limits on where everything suddenly stop. You are the end everything reaches. I might be the growth and decay, the change and all good and bad between. But you are the final set of the sun, you are the void and the silence of death."

"I never liked how you saw the universe as a set of variables that had to be perfectly adjusted for everything to work. But I respected it, and I admired you and will to do what's needed."

"Then you lost your mind and started to wonder if you were really needed for all of this to exist."

Her claw lunch down to the Everlight's face black shadows burning divine skin drawing out a cosmic scene painted in the blood of a Guardian.

That first hit from the Everchanging missed the strength to seriously hurt the other, contained by a dim string of remaining love for her sister. But it was a resolve and with all her wrath contained in growing sphere of live and decay, a light of mortality was to struck the everlight.

Just to suddenly stop and disappear as the anger of the Everchanging turned into growing horror.

Hair burn by the first strike revealed a hidden eye.
