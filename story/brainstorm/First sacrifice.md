# First sacrifice

tags:: Story/Brainstorm
- The dragon of shadows, the guardian of chaos smirks as she spy the lives of those touched by the light, she watches as their silly short lives run with an illusion of control, as if their lives weren't the result of something way beyond them.
- They are characters to her, playing a plot that repeats infinitely, everytime with different voices and faces.
- Sometimes thought the characters did something unexpected, and suddenly the characters became people, people she learned to cherish.
- Upon death she picks her chosen ones, her favorites and gives them an option, to continue to the other life or follow her through the darkness.
- Your eyelids feel heavy, and your body heavy as stone, the world is tinted in red, darkening and darkening, until the endless darkness claims you. Your life is slipping away from your fingers, every second is slower and it seems to extend into infinity, the world tells you to give up, you know it would be easier, if you just let the darkness claim you, become one with it and the song that extends into infinity.
- It may be stubbornness, the wound in your body is mortal, and death is not merciful of your wish to live, but you still fight against the weight on your eyes, against the shadows growing on your sight and the peace and bliss they promise you in the form of a lullaby an impossible song of infinite beauty, something you just know cannot be heard by mortal ears, the notes are impossible, something you would have never dream to ever hear. And it makes the pain of living insufferable, you want to join the harmony of something way bigger than you, accept their embrace, dissolve into it till there’s nothing left of what you would call an identity.
- Yet you fight it, yet you live an eternity in a second, just so you don’t join the melody becoming louder and louder, slowly weakening your will.
- But as you are sure you will give up to the melody, you see her, a shadow, infinitely darker than the shadows of death, yet infinitely more alive, you feel her smiling at you as her voice joins the melody.
- And her voice is life. The roar of thunder, the breeze of summer, the cry of war and the laughter of children, is hope, is despair, is suffering and longing, fire and the crack of ice, is the voice of a mother and the cruelest of monster, is victory and defeat is chaos overwhelming deaths harmony… a song that you never heard but deep in your soul you always known.
- A reminder and a demand, she calls you to fight for the beautiful yet horror filled song of life.
- She offers her hand to your soul, claws of ever changing shadows melting into soft silky skin.
- Just moving your own hand to meet hers feels like fighting a war, the harmony weighs on you begging you to stay, but you win.
- And as soon as you touch her hand the world fills with pain, spears strike straight through your soul, you burn in the flames of hell, you freeze as if you were in the void of space.
- You suffer unimaginable pain yet when you hear her voice once more. You know, you just know. It all is worth it for life gives back what you suffer for ten fold.
- You feel The weight of your body  as your eyes open again on top of charcoal sand that extends to a path into infinity, surrounded by the violent sound of an ocean and a black sky barely illuminated by far away stars
- Tears run down your face as you see yourself standing on your two feet, feeling the sand under your weight as you take the first steps into the infinite path.
- As you walk you find a child, a boy with pure white hair and black and sharp wings, he asks you a question without giving you context and walks aways.
- You find a second and a third as con continue, the “nyphen” looking more and more like Daemons and straight up monsters. the seven you find, a woman wearing a mask, grows wings from her face and cries blood, as her body breaks and twitches growing arms and claws, eyes and needle feathers.
- She don’t ask, she just tells you
- “Keep your heart cold and your goals clear, once you speak with the devil. You may never escape the darkness of her wings”
- you continue and the beach disappears, thousands of eyes stare back at you judging, and a smile approaches you, another woman, or so she looks like, you know is not real, the shadows surrounds her, paper white skin turns into pitch black scales, black and sharp claws turn into thin and delicate fingers. Her red lips grow fangs. Only the smile is constant.
- "Welcome to my home."
