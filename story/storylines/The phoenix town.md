- The phoenix town is a town that is everywhere and nowhere at the same time.
  
   It's the place that is said to hold the most powerful and beautiful phoenixes out there. The phoenix town is a place that may appear on your travels after you surpass a hardship or during so. It's a place that helps you, it lets you rest and recover, so you can continue on your way.
  
  But its apparitions is not something that occurs to anyone. Those who find the town are said to be special, selected by the town itself to be part of them. You will enter, they will take care of you, they will ask you a lot of questions. Questions hidden in conversations. What do you want to do more forward? How do you feel about the heat? Do you want to grow better? Do you want to be better?
  
  These questions aim for a single thing. If you want to become a phoenix just like them. Phoenix that are born from being chosen by the call. Those who hear the town's call are stronger than normal phoenixes. They get a stat higher. They get an extra ability combined into their already racials. Phoenixes who are already phoenixes and find the town also grow stronger.
