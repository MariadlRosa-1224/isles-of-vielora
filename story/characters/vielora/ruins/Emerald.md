	- ## Background
	- Emerald was found abandoned as a baby by [[the mayor of Ruined City]] . The man took her under his protection as his daughter despite not being a remnant. It was hard at the start, but the people of the ruins learned to love her, love her charismatic personality, love the way she didn't seem to bother about what anybody think about her or the remnants. She was a joy that the city needed a joy her father needed, as she grew older and she was presented to the world, she was discouraged and treated badly because of the people who raised her, mocked for not being annoyed in the slightest by the aura all remnants seems to have around them. She was called a Winged remain. And she didn't care. She in fact like that title. It made her feel that she belonged. And despite what people told her, she took all of that and called a reason why people shouldn know what the ruined city was all about, what remnants were really all about. She tried and still tries to make people love their city despite all the pre-sumptions and the racisms.

	- But it would be a lie if it didn’t bother her sometimes, she is sharper than what she let shows. Her father was honest with her, she knew she was abandoned or at least she was found alone as a baby in the middle of the frozen ruins. A place where she would have died if the mayor didn't find her This make her a connection with the place. She likes to stay there, even if it's cold, even if it's dangerous. She calls this place, the place where nothing is born but everything is reborn.
