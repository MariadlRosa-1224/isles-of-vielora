- ## Background
- Chaos is change, the guardian of growth and decay but not of stops, no matter how gruesome her concept can get to be, she is not death, In the contrary, Eos is life, she is everything alive and moving, the burst of flames, the beating heart, the healing and the disease.
- That is what mom told me the first time I met Eos, she told me that before I even got to see her, and I understand why, from afar it was hard to see a “she”, what I saw was the giant wings of a dragon, a moving shadow with horns, scales and claws. Towering over me and mom.
- It was once she turned around that the vision changed; there stood woman, beautiful one by any who ignores the pointy parts; giant clawed hands and dragon feet, paper white skin with black patches of scaled skin, fangs, lizard like blood eyes, and the way she moved like a stalking predator, half the time with limbs on the floor and two tails standing high and proud.
-
- Get over that and you see a sharp and dangerous yet sweet looking face, a tender expression, an slender yet worked body, muscular in all the right places, juicy red lips colored in bright red, skin soft to the touch in any part away from the scales, soft hair where flowers grow as a crown on her head. Tidings around impressive horns.
