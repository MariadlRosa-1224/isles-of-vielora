- All eldritch gods are, well, eldritch, but not all eldritch creatures are gods.
- They are referred to by many names, monsters, demons, devils, etc, they inhabit the void and the inverted world.
- The void is an empty space in the ordered universe, abandoned for the lack of sun and planets to bring life to become perfect hideouts for eldritch outposts.
- Eldritch could be seen as the archons of chaos, or the blessed by chaos, they aren’t of any set race so appearances vary a lot, from giant monsters with tentacles and millions of eyes, to the stereotype demons and devils to anything imaginable and inimaginable.
- There are a few common characteristics though:
- 1. they are either extremely powerful or have an extremely powerful ability
- 2. They are shapeshifters.
- 3. They have a “true appearance” that can mess with the minds of those who look at it unprepared.
- 4. They have the power to break the minds of others showing them the purest form of chaos
- 5. they only follow the others of more powerful eldritch, eldritch gods and the guardian of chaos.
- Fallen are a type of eldritch.
