- "I still remember what it was like to be human, I might be the only one who truly remembers, the Remnants were reborn into a new form to no remember who their souls once were. Those who escaped into space, they are probably long since gone if the words of the dragon of shadows are true and **she...** despite her body she was never human, no matter what she says, I know she can not truly understand.
  
  I remember, the time I was two, two names, two bodies, two species... two souls.
  
  My body, my human half, was frail, I was a pure human, unable to use magic, but free from any chain to divinity, the wonder of gods.
  
  But, I didn't feel free, never while living among humans, very early in the war, when the aggressions were hidden and unknown to most eyes, I was an assassin, human who killed humans and was paid for it. My life was luxurious but I was eternally on the run, settle down was the same as waiting to be caught, either by the police or by the countless enemies I made. I was not the gods image of the wonderful humanity they came to earth for.
  
  But everything changed when I met the crow clan, when I met him, my other half.
  
  I still remember what was to be an angel, not a nyphen, an angel was created to serve humans, Nyphen were born free of them in the new world, all of us fallen remember that, and if the dragon of shadows is right, we are the only ones who remember
  
  As a being of pure magic, I was created to serve humanity, powerful, but with my power locked behind the chain of an order woven inside our souls. We were never free, but that didn't mean I didn't like my life, or ever felt like a prisoner or slave, at least no at the start, when we were ignorant of what happened in the darkest parts of the human world. My clan was one of those who didn't serve humanity directly, living secluded and helping those who we found on our way. Our lives were simple, not as glamorous or filled with technology as the human cities that existed back them, but we were happy. Even if we weren't the all the submissive angels the gods planned us to be.
  
  One day a human stumble in front of us, she seemed to be escaped from someone, a laser-shot in the lower half of her abdomen with blood running out of it, a bag full of money and weapons, and probably the most tired out and hopeless eyes I had ever seen.
  
  We hid her from her captors and healed her wounds with our magic. When she woke up, I saw a little light inside those darkened eyes. I didn't know back then, but that day I met who became my other half.
