# Daemons
- index:: [[Race]]
- Originally they were believed to be Nyxkins as they share some similarities with them and their origin was also not one from the world or the gods. But that's not true, daemons are races that are born from evil, destruction and negative feelings. They themselves aren't necessarily evil, but it is common among these races to have more wicked and violent tendencies, bringing chaos wherever they go. These races are often looked with a very negative eye or even hunted.
