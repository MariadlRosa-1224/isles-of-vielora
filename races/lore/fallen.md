# Fallen

**Origin** Ethereal (chaos touched)

**Base abilities:**

- **Nyphen origin** They posses all the abilities expected from a Nyphen but greatly stronger.

- **Shapeshift** They can change their appearance completely at will. The only thing always unchanged is their broken halo, but that can change in size to match their form.

**Description:**

Some would describe them as broken and terrifying versions of a nyphen. Some describe them as holders of a beauty beyond comprehension and some describe them as nyphen but dark.

Appearance varies among all of them and records say they could even change their appearance at will, with a few common features.

They have giant wings that have been seen in many colors, with black as the most common. A black broken halo on top of their heads and an aura able to make the strongest kneel before them.
