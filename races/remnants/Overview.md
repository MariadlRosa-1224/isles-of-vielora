# Remnants

The universe was used to beings without a soul, golems, dolls and artifacts that with magic alone were given the ability to feel and have a will, but not a soul.

A soul is a thing that only gods can grant and a soul created by a god is eternally bound to its creator.

Humans were believed to be the same, soulless beings that were granted consciousness via the very complex evolutionary process that made them in the first place. The reason why they couldn’t use magic and why they didn’t believe nor serve gods in the first place.

A soul is a mysterious thing that not even gods understand, like making something out of clay, you can make clay, you can use clay, doesn’t mean you understand the specifics of their composition or why it acts like it does.

But turns out humans had souls all along, the strange and impossible ability to create souls of their own, despite not being bound to anyone.

And when earth was destroyed the souls of the unfortunate unable to escape earth were also trapped in the ruins of the planet unable to move on, trapped in the area where the attack first impacted, ironically the place with the most well preserved structure and artifacts of the past

Some souls, particularly those culprits of the destruction of earth for their evils became corrupted, monsters roaming the ruins searching to cause death and pain in those who enter their territory.

Most were given another chance, their bodies morphed into something that wasn’t but resembled what humans were in the past and what they created and did.

The definition of a remain that most people know is of beings who resemble, are believed or are known to come from the times of humanity

Another lesser known definition is of beings who inherited a human soul and/or their ability to create a human-like soul for themselves and their descendants without the intervention of a god.
