- # Living Toy
  Race:: Remnants
	- **Origin:** [[Remnants]]
	- **Base abilities:**
		- **Core** As long as their core remains intact, they can have any wound repaired.
		- **Modification** A toy can get their body modified with new pieces and add-ons.
		- **Painless** because of how they are made living toys are unable to feel physical pain or tiredness, they can be torn apart ragged in multitude of pieces, as long as their core is intact they can recover and continue moving. Or even control independently the torn parts of themselves.
	- **Unlockables:**
		- **(level x:)**
			- **Patchwork Bond** Some Living Toys can form a magical link with a person they deeply care for. This link allows them to feel their bonded partner’s emotions even at a distance, and they instinctively know when that person is in danger. While near them, the toy becomes more durable x2 in their physical and magical, and their presence can reduce the pain or fear their partner feels. It’s not unusual for such toys to even glow faintly when protecting their bonded person.
		- **level x:**
			- **Puffed with Air** Some Toys are so light they can float a short distance when thrown or fall very slowly, gently gliding like a feather. If caught by wind or magic, they might even spin in the air like a leaf.
			- **Velcro grip** This Toy sticks to things a little too well, attaching into any surface they won't fall unless they want or are pulled by something twice their strength.
			- **Voice mimic** Some can imitate any voice they’ve heard before—perfectly. This can be used for mimicry, trickery, or even comfort. A Toy might replicate a loved one’s voice to calm someone or play eerie tricks by speaking as someone long gone.
		- **Level x**
			- **Nursing** Some toys can pull their mana outwards to create a healing area Everyone on it will get their wounds healed and  minds feeling calmer. over the course of 2 turn healing minor and moderate wounds, it consumes 3 charges per turn after the second one.
			- **hide and seek** Some toys are able to teleport small distances (up to 15 meters) within sight, as long as there aren't obstacles in between, useful to dodge attacks or reaching places they are normally unable to. This toys are also nearly undetectable in their movement, making no sound and hiding under c rank detection or lower. Each time they teleport they spent 5 charges and they cannot attack the same turn they teleport.
			- **Mana surge:** Living toys aren't made for combat but some are able to overcharge they core with mana to greatly increase their capabilities temporary getting temporary a x6 in their speed, strength, and reaction speed. This last for 6 turns with a 10 turn cooldown. The sight of these toys fighting is quite comical.
		- **Level X**
			- **Toy creator** Some living toys can create and manipulate up to 8 toys of their liking or manipulate those around them. These toys doesn't have any will only to obey their creator and can be made of any material and given upgrades by their creator.
		- **Level X**
			- **Soul Toy** Some toys, even if they weren't made with one, develop a soul naturally over time. these toys have new capabilities such as being able to see lingering spirits and people's true nature and have a x4 boost in their magic.
	- **Description:** They take the appearance of toys, dolls, plushies, etc. Generally they are small, less than half a meter but there are records of they getting as big as 2 meter. Aside of the usual appearance of the toy they are based in they have a small core incrusted in some place of their body or as an accessory.
	- **weaknesses:**
		- **Core** If the core is destroyed they will die.
		- **Soulless** For good or bad a living toy is unaffected by soul effects.
	- **History:**
		- Living toys are a very old race, said to be first created in the times of humans when powerful mages started experimenting with mana, giving life to inanimate objects. Such as toys.
		  
		  This practice although very rare and only done by experienced mages, is still done nowadays. Toys are usually made as companions for children of rich families or as loyal servants of a fun size. This give them a kind and playful nature that added to they cuteness make them a well liked race. And despite their origin they are treated normally.
		  
		  Some living toys though are made with actual soul, these ones don't remember their previous lives or have very vague memories, this practice is heavily prohibited and taboo is very unlikely you find a toy made like this.
	- **Lifespan:** unaging
