Race:: Ethereals

- # Nyphen
  
  **Origin** Ethereal 
  
  **Base abilities:**
	- **The servant:**  Nyphen were created to be perfect servants, able to cooperate in any task asked by a human, for this purpose their bodies and minds were made strong, stronger that for most might look at first glance,  x3 in all physical and mental stats, x5 speed when flying.
	  id:: 68119514-88c4-4133-bb2a-31275a7a3007
	-
	- **Being of magic:** Nyphen were only made to look human, but they are hardly beings of flesh and bone, their bodies are almost completely made of mana, shaped to imitate the looks, functions and weakness of the human body, they don't really need to breath, and can survive months without food or water as long as they have access to mana, this grant them x4 in magic output and defense, +2 in mana charges and mana regeneration.
	-
	- **Light affinity:** able to manipulate light to a degree make it change colors, allows the nyphen to create holograms, illuminate areas, some tricks and give soft warm, is inoffensive, the worst it can do is harmlessly flashing someone’s face. They also get an additional x2 in light related magic.
	-
	- **Ethereal presence:** In the presence of an nyphen people feel a peaceful yet pressurizing presence, nyphen are different and everyone can feel it, their sole presence feel otherworldly, yet is peaceful and calming, no one can really feel threatened by them. May cause involuntary doubt and hesitation when attacking them.
- **Unlockables:**
  
  **level 5:**
	- **The Beauty:** Some nyphen grow to be greatly beautiful, a work of art made a person with unnatural grace in their movement and voices, their wings wrapping around them and changing to be fluffier and glow faintly with magic. Graceful nyphen have a gust of feathers spinning around them, capable of flying without the flap of their wings they float slightly off the ground and when moving glide without movement of their legs. When flying they can choose to hover and fall slowly with grace little races can replicate.
	- **The Caring:** Some nyphen grow to be greatly empathetic and perceptive, a caring precense of protection with a calming touch and voice. Becoming capable of feeling the vibe or mood of others around them if the emotion is particularly strong. Even affecting their taste buds.
	- **The Curious:** Some nyphen grow to be greatly intelligent and curious, sharp eyes, able to perceive what others cannot and a voice that speaks unspoken knowledge. The Nyphen can implant an insight into an ally’s mind and the ally suddenly "realizes" a piece of useful, but non-obvious, information like a safer route or a password. They can only offer insight of the present and this can only be used twice per ally, per roleplay, cannot be used on themselves.
- **level 10:**
	- **The Grace: (Requires The Beauty)** A grace of songs and dances that empower allies, for as long as they dance or sing up to 4 allies around them get a x3 boost in their physical stats. But it will stop as soon as they are interrupted.
	- **The Kindness: (Requires The Caring)** Some nyphen become kind, capable of making a shine from their mana which they launch into the air before it locks onto a target. Giving the mana to the person it's locked onto over the span of 5 turns. Only one can be made and has a 10 turn cooldown.
	- **The Perception: (Requires The Curious)** The Nyphen no longer perceives the world with their senses alone. Instead, their awareness unfolds outward and *inhabits others*. When they are near, allies find their senses heightened x4 their original, their awareness shared like a web that grows with the more people in it allowing to share their thoughts and senses. But the more people the more overwhelming it becomes for the nyphen to handle all of them, managing more people than the multiplier of their mental strength will cause them to faint after 3 turns.
- **level 15:**
	- **The Elegance: (Requires The Grace)** A performance of serene power, *The Elegance* is a song or dance that shields, calms, and uplifts those around the nyphen. As they begin to move or sing, their presence becomes radiant and soothing. Allies who step through the path they’ve traced feel a sudden relief—a clarity of mind and body. Minor injuries seem dulled, movement steadied, and minds focused. Reducing the effect of any negative emotion alteration.
	-
	- **The Compassion: (Requires The Caring)** Through sensing of others a nyphen becomes capable of seeing a person's condition and ailments, both good or bad and how much stamina and mana they have left. Vision working up to B rank ailments/curses. They becoming able to heal up to c rank.
	-
	- **The Guide: (Requires The Perception)** A curious Nyphen is a guide meant to lead others to their destiny. the guide allows Nyphen to perceive outside his presence allowing them to point at the general direction where something or someone willing to be found is when asked. This only works on goals lower than B rank and causes confusion if there are multiple of the same goals or they aren't clear enough for the Nyphen.

- **level 20:**
	- **Open eyes:** Suddenly the Nyphen feels lighter as if an invisible constraint was lifted, their magic no longer is only for the sake of others they can finally use their magic and abilities for themselves. The effects of all previous racials can also apply to the nyphen and now and they can use spells runes and other forms of magic that benefits themselves.

- **level 25:**
	- **The Dancer: (Requires The Elegance)** Dancers are stronger they get a x5 boost in their physical stats instead of the normal x3. With wind in their step some nyphen take to dance to express strength, gliding along the floor when dancing a nyphen cause a 8 meter area around them to become wind charged. magic output getting a 2x boost and causing others to glide along the floor like themselves without the ability to fly.
	-
	- **The Nurture: (Requires The Compassion)** from hating to see the burden of others kind nyphen learn to take the burden from others. When a ally within 10 meters takes damage the nyphen can choose to shift the damage onto themselves if magical or mental, gaining +3x to the defense of the type of damage and +2x regeneration. With a 5 turn cooldown once done.

	- **The Insight: (Requires the Guide)** Their focus strengthens, these nyphen thirst for knowledge, their memory becoming photographic in nature and with intense focus can use magic to comb through text and implant the knowledge onto themselves, gaining minor telekinetic abilities, enough to lift a few pounds.

- **level 30:**

	-
	- **The Shelter: (Requires The Nurture)** Nyphen are capable of making a barrier around someone's body, this shield scaling with their defense, upon being hit the shield is spent and gives the person who hit it a 4x debuff in the stat used to hit them. Debuff lasts 5 turns with a 10 turn cooldown.

	- **The Wisdom: (Requires The Insight)** Increases mental stats x4. This ability projects outwards, suppressing negative emotions and mental ailments (magical or otherwise) in others, granting allies a x4 in mental defense for 5 turns. Affects Fauna (B Rank or lower):  Induces either a state of docility or fear in non-aggressive creatures.

Their magic is no longer constrained to only be harmless helpers. They can use it to defend themselves, to grant disadvantages to others, yet their own abilities still cannot cause true harm.

- **Description:**
- Nyphen were made to look mostly human. Their great difference are their wings, colors, halos and how beautiful they always look. Their wings can vary a lot in shape, size and color. But they are never black. Many nyphen have small wings and feathers in different parts of their body. Still a single pair of full wings is what is most commonly seen. Having 2 or 3 pairs is incredibly rare, a sight only reserved to the most powerful of nyphen. But anything beyond 3 is unheard, only a thing of legends, of divine beings and of monsters.
- **weaknesses:**
- **An order written in the soul** Nyphens were made to be unable to harm humans, and although they are gone, there is still a consequence of that condition. A Nyphen cannot develop abilities that would directly harm others, and offenssive magic they use find itself unable to cause direct harm to people, only being able to create supporting abilities that benefit other people but not themselves.

- **History:**
-
- Once upon a time they were called angels, gifts from the gods to humanity inspired by their religions and legends. Beautiful, Powerful, magical, kind.
  
  They lived with humanity for many years in a sort of harmony.They brought magic to their technological driven world; they were a revolution.
  
  Until it wasn’t. Humanity declare to them a war they couldn’t fight.
-
- On paper they were stronger, a magic on pair to some of humans strongest weapons and wings able to resist bullets. But they were unable to do it. A handicap given in their creation, they were unable to cause harm to humans. At least Not without going through extreme paralyzing pain.
-
- It was a war lost from the start. They prayed, begged their gods, to save them. But nobody came
  
  But at the end of everything there wasn’t a winner. Humanity was gone, and so were the gods. The only thing left for the nyphen was
  a dying planet and the frozen ruins of a once beautiful home. And for 3 thousand years that was all they had
-
- It's been ten thousand years since then, through the passing of generation, many decided to move on from the past, accept the apology of the new gods an join other races in a new society nyphen are still a secluded race with rooted reservations against gods, their history is a scar that can be forgiven but never forgotten.
