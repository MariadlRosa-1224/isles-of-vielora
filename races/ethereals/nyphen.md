- **Description:**
- Nyphen were made to look mostly human. Their great difference are their wings, colors, halos and how beautiful they always look. Their wings can vary a lot in shape, size and color. But they are never black. Many nyphen have small wings and feathers in different parts of their body. Still a single pair of full wings is what is most commonly seen. Having 2 or 3 pairs is incredibly rare, a sight only reserved to the most powerful of nyphen. But anything beyond 3 is unheard, only a thing of legends, of divine beings and of monsters.
- **weaknesses:**
- **An order written in the soul** Nyphens were made to be unable to harm humans, and although they are gone, there is still a consequence of that condition. A Nyphen cannot develop abilities that would directly harm others, and offenssive magic they use find itself unable to cause direct harm to people, only being able to create supporting abilities that benefit other people but not themselves.

- **History:**
-
- Once upon a time they were called angels, gifts from the gods to humanity inspired by their religions and legends. Beautiful, Powerful, magical, kind.
  
  They lived with humanity for many years in a sort of harmony.They brought magic to their technological driven world; they were a revolution.
  
  Until it wasn’t. Humanity declare to them a war they couldn’t fight.
-
- On paper they were stronger, a magic on pair to some of humans strongest weapons and wings able to resist bullets. But they were unable to do it. A handicap given in their creation, they were unable to cause harm to humans. At least Not without going through extreme paralyzing pain.
-
- It was a war lost from the start. They prayed, begged their gods, to save them. But nobody came
  
  But at the end of everything there wasn’t a winner. Humanity was gone, and so were the gods. The only thing left for the nyphen was
  a dying planet and the frozen ruins of a once beautiful home. And for 3 thousand years that was all they had
-
- It's been ten thousand years since then, through the passing of generation, many decided to move on from the past, accept the apology of the new gods an join other races in a new society nyphen are still a secluded race with rooted reservations against gods, their history is a scar that can be forgiven but never forgotten.
