- # Ghost
- Race:: Graveborns
-
- **Origin:**  [Graveborn]([[Race/Graveborns]])
  
  **size:** Normal
  
  **Base abilities:**
- **Magic mastery:** They have a natural mastery over magic, as it is their very body, ghosts gets a x5 in magic
- **Eyes of a soul:** Ghost are able to see and communicate with other lingering spirits. They get the ability to perceive the soul of another person and see its nature.
- **Mana being:** Ghosts have a second set of mana charges that is always 20% the max amount of charges in their main set, starting a 10 charges, if this second set is emptied the ghost will fade. It will only start draining if the first set is drained.
- **untouchable:** Ghost cannot touch or be touched nor attacked via physical means. Barriers, and weapons unless imbued with mana can also be ignored by them
-
- **Unlockables:**
  
  **level x:** 
  
  **Electrical disturbance:** As a ghosts presence becomes more powerful some develop the ability to disrupt electronics, their mana distorting technology both magical and electrical. Getting more powerful the stronger the ghost is and how close they are, ranging from dimming of lights to the device not functioning if the ghost phases through it. While controllable if angered this effect becomes unstable.
  
  **Invisibility** Some ghosts are capable of becoming invisible, this special invisibility scaling to B rank at the cost of Phasing through objects or contact leaving residue visible with mana sight.
  
  **Phantom hands:** Rather than pure telekinesis ghosts can summon phantom hands to pick up items and people, these hands scaling to 25% of their strength. Ghosts capable of making 10 of these hands within a 10 meter area of themselves. 
  
  **level x:**
  
  **Phantom shrill:** A ghosts vocal chords can become naturally infused with magic, causing their voice to gain new properties. They can project their voice up to 20 meters around them in a location and they change how it sounds. Their most powerful weapon being using it to scream, this shrill is high pitched and at the threshold of pain. The shrill lasting 3 turns before going on an 8 turn cooldown. It can be projected but if done has a 1 turn moment before blasting. Each consecutive turn the vibrations get more potent both useful for damage and defense.
  
  **level x:**
  
  **The torment** Some ghosts become vengeful, capable of entering someone and flooding their body with their own mana now twisted and warped to place a curse on them. The curse isn't too bad at first but overtime the person will feel sick, hearing voices and seeing images of the ghost in twisted forms and states, landscape twisting and becoming grotesque to the point even their own reflection shows something else. Their body will twitch and convulse the longer than Its active, Strong enough mental resistance or curse resistance can break it but it's quite subtle to detect if used, only way to tell before it activates being very strong mana sight.
  
  **The mischief** Using mana charges they can make objects move on their own, making them act and do basic movements to annoy others, in addition within a 3 meter area of themselves they can fling loose objects and make them float, the force of the launch depending on their own strength.
  
  **The protector** Some ghosts become protectors to the ones they love, capable of breaking curses and debuff, and even freeing others from control by possessing them and fighting to destroy what ails them, doesn't work on everything but becomes more effective the stronger the ghost is.
  
  **level x:**
  
  **Spiritual link (Requires the protector)** Link themself to a person strengthening both of them but at the cost that both will share the wounds of the person they linked themselves. If the target is willing it allows temporary possession, an stage in which the ghost can use the host abilities while in control of the body. This cannot be forced and if the host no longer wants the ghost inside it will be expelled.
  
  **The cursed (Requires the torment)** Some ghosts are particularly destructive, become twisted by dark mana. They can enter a body and inject their secondary set of mana into the victim, dark mana. The more mana injected the stronger it's effects. Sickness and weakening of the body in addition to vomiting, consuming their mana to the point it becomes unrechargable unless cured with x rank magic as if a poison of that rank. Although powerful the ghost needs to inhabit the victim to do this leaving them vulnerable especially if the person chooses to damage themselves, dealing 50% additional damage.
  
  **Free spirit (Requires the mischief)** Free spirits can hardly be anchored freely changing their looks and able to phase through magic barriers up to b rank. They can now fragment themselves into multiple "echoes" of themselves that can traverse 20 meters around the ghost and freely change appearance. 
  
  **level x**
  
  **Soul touch** More than just see them, a ghost eventually becomes able to interact with the very soul, their touch, gentle or aggressive, it lash at you, not your body, not your mana, not your mind, **you**, its very intent is physically felt. A ghost can understand others, connect with their true selves and heal beyond anything else. But they can also harm beyond anything else. 
  
  A ghost can harm and heal a soul, but a soul is still hard to affect, their magic leaving subtle effects, leaving and healing the tiniest cuts. These cuts leave no lasting impact or damage but are immensely painful as if you were being ripped apart at your core, even when subsiding it feels as if unmade wounds remain.
  
  Doing this though is extremely draining, a single spell or ability directed to the soul, will consume 20% of the mana charges of the ghost
- , no matter how many they have.
  
  **level x:**
  
  **High spirit** makes the ghost more powerful, strengthening their very soul, the soul gaining new potential. 2 chosen abilities will fuse into one, getting a 50% boost in power and way more versatility as traits are smashed together. (The power of the 2 abilities is added and averages before the 50% is applies.)

- **Description:** 
  id:: 68465920-0da2-4d5c-923e-0b0d2addfc29
  
  They are a glowing echo of who they truly felt they were in life. Their shapes are wispy, a moving cloud of light, not quite solid. Within this ethereal form, all sorts of colors swirl and pulse, changing to represent what the ghost is feeling at any moment, their appearance tend to change in the course of their afterlife as their perception changes.
  
  **weaknesses:**
- **Mana dependant** If all the mana charges of a ghost are drained, it will fade.
-
- **magic drain** When hit by magic attack their mana charges are drained in proportion of the mana cost of the attack rather than its power. When hit with a magic attack, even one that doesn't cost mana and therefore can’t do actual damage, they can feel it as if it did.
-
- **vulnerable soul** Without a body to protect their souls, they are more vulnerable to hazards that can damage it.
-
- **History:**
  
  A ghost is a lingering soul who decided they are no ready to rest nor reincarnate, so they roam the world sustained only by their mana and will.
  
  Reasons why a ghost sticks around varies, love, revenge, an unfinished business, or maybe just thinking eternal rest sounds boring.
  
  Ghosts are often threated with respect many seeing them as a miracle that allow them to stay longer with goner loved ones, families having their elders as ghosts roaming around aren't rare.
