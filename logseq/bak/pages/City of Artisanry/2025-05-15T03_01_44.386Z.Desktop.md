- Goverment_god:: Artisanry
- The city of Artisanry is one of the main capitals of the world. This is a city where Invertors, artisans and everyone related come together to discuss their creations. It’s placed on the Badlands, a place filled with many materials and mines, where most materials are collected. After the ruins, it's the second settlement for minerals in the world; it would be the most popular if the ruins weren't so ludicrous despite the dangers. This city is like Vegas, always awake with lights all the time, but instead of casinos, it is filled with inventions. Whole buildings used for experiments, and streets are filled with people showing off what they believe might be the next great invention. If you want to go to the capital of innovation, go to the city of Artisanry, which combines technology and art to create something great, something new. Everyone is very welcome here, and there are many popular activities you may try.
