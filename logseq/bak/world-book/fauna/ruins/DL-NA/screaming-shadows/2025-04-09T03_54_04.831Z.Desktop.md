## Screaming shadow

**Rarity:** common

**Danger level:** 0

**description:** Screaming shadows are created by withered mana. They are all over the ruins, shadowing figures of multiple sizes and shapes that stalk the wanderers who dare to enter the ruins. They are harmless but as they aren't real beings but a manifestation of withered mana, they cannot be killed. Only there to distract wanderers from the true dangers of the ruins.

**Abilities:**
- Endless scream
- teleportation
- Darkness creation
  
  
  **Region:** The ruins
  
  **Tameability:** untameable
