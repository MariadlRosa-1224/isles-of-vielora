- The door to hell. It is placed in the lowest and sourdest part of the ruins. An Island that from the distance seems pretty normal, aside from the giantstatues of two wings that stands off its edge, there seems to be nothing around them. There seems to be no reason why, aside of the unnatural looking formation, this place should be any different. Except it is, somehow, when you get to there.
- The ruins is always gloomy, but this place once you're standing there seems to be always in the dark, no matter the hour or how is the climate outside, and the storm disappears as soon as you leave the island, it's always raining too. Puddles of water fill the place. So maybe bring an umbrella.
- And then you are in front of them two wings standing tall from the ground and a light that seems to form in between them, making anything behind them invisible. This light also seems to disappear as soon as you leave the island. You could try to move around the wings. You could try to bypass the door. To bypass the light. There's no use. It seems to be impossible to cross.
- This place will be important when Core Island arrives. The old explorer announces that the door has opened, the exploration of the core Island will stand once more, and it's recruiting people, those who feel they are strong enough to survive life, this cruel adventure, those who are willing to risk it all for glory, come to the hell door, because we are about to explore a place that could very well be hell.
