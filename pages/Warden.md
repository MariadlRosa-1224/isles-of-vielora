  **Information:** Wardens are spirit-like humanoid creatures of unknown origin and motives. They are known to be sentient but will not talk and refuse to communicate with anyone in any way. They appear in moments of great disaster to protect the population from menaces going as far as to revive the innocent who has fallen. For this reason, they are treated with great respect and even worshiped by the general population, and the simple act of attacking or insulting them is considered a crime.
  
  Their main settlements are called "warden cities". Places protected by a power beyond those of the wardens. They can be seen patrolling the city frequently, but trying to get close to them will only lead to them disappearing.
