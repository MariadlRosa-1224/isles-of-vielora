- Name: Vampirism 
  Type: [[Sickness]]
- Effects: Aggression, hunger for blood, progressive changing into a vampire, weakness, confusion.
- Description: When a vampire hits a certain level of strength their black blood becomes vampiric, concentrated with enough vampiric cells for it to be capable of turning others into vampires if they come into contact with enough of it. The stronger the vampire the less blood is needed for infection. For vampires just reaching this point full transformation takes weeks while for powerful vampires it can take less than a day. Once someone changes they are a vampire and cannot be cured by most means. Vampirism can only be given to races with little non humanoid traits.
- duration: N/A
- Healing: Healing magic or cleansing magic as strong or stronger than the vampire who infected, potions.
