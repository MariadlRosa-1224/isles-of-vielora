- Name: Murites Mambium
  Type: [[Sickness]]
- Effects: Vomiting of blue liquid, out of control regeneration, mana drain, blue coloration on body, weakness, general unwellness.
- Description: A parasitic substance that feeds and multiplies on a person's mana, causing it to pool in their body and be vomited out to infect another. If untreated can be lethal but isn't particularly fast, when affected the person's regenerative capabilities are vastly increased. Mambium can treated and harvested into a medicine usable in combat for healing similar to a healing potion without the cooldown, but overexposure can cause overdose and becoming infected.
- duration: Short if potions, N/A if infected.
- Healing: Killing or removal of Mambium from body, B rank cleansing.
