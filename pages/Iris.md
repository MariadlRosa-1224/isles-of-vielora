	  background-color:: gray
		- Is hard to tell when exactly she was born, it was when or will be when her halves reach their destiny, did she exist since the start of everything as the ripples and emergent connections that formed the first vestiges of the soul and therefore, Alma? Or maybe it was the moment when mom first awoken their consciousness in her curiosity to understand the only thing outside of her infinite knowledge? Maybe it was the moment where her existence as herself became an inevitability, tied to a promise made by the remnants of a God made to a family whose only wish was to see their precious child survive the winters of a cruel war.
		- But as uncertain as her origin is, there is no denying to what she is. The ripple in a calm ocean of certainty, the moment when all laws of creation break to give origin to all that cannot be predicted, the meaning behind the countless melodies that will ever and never exist, the tears that fall from countless eyes that stare into infinity, the doubt that makes empires collapse and the shared silence that will rebuild them anew. She is the absurdity of imagination that turns impossibility into reality, the eternal observant and the single yet ever growing infinite crowd of witnesses of a beautiful creation, never meant but made for them. All centered not by a core, magic or physical component, but by the result of freedom, the possibility not even a God could predict and made the universe a kaleidoscope infinitely more beautiful it could have ever been.
