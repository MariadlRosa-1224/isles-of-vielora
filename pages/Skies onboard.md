# Skies onboard
	- *You have won a ticket to join the skies onboard – an experience many people say you should live at least once in your life.*
	  id:: c4c9a581-2c5e-440e-a409-eab33b4cd213
	- *It's a flying ship that travels all around the planet, teaching you about many small things from different places, and the many things you could get to do there.*
	- *There are many activities inside the ship. You could go and hear about different legends; you could sit and fight with others; you could even be one of the first people to test the holograms, where you fight with fake big monsters and are rewarded with small trinkets.*
	- *This event showcases different mechanics of the world. It introduces the world as a whole, showing players the different things they can do in the hope that they get interested.*
	- *It is also an event made to collect feedback, as it is the very first of the beta events. People can give feedback about what they think about the races, the combat mechanics, and the stats. Everything is free to change at this point. Of course, most things were already being considered, so it's not like we'll make a whole overhaul, but big changes are still allowed.*

	- ## Story
		- ### Prelude
			- As this is the very first event even before the main history, the only things that will come before this are the history of the world. How did we get here? About the history it will be talked a bit about in the event, talking about how the world has been somewhat peaceful, the limitations, how space seems to be inaccessible even though the technology to get outside already existing. This kind of little mystery is that try to get people into doing something. After all, the important thing is to teach people that here you can experiment, do whatever you want.
			  id:: 681ae368-6aa7-4964-bc37-1e554687c734
		- ### Main part
			- You find yourself on a ship, a flying ship, taking a ride all over the world. Inside the ship, there are many activities to do. You can fight holographic monsters. You can hear about the legends of the world. You can listen to various NPCs talking about their cities and promoting different activities available there. You can go around and eavesdrop on conversations. More often than not, they will be talking about something interesting. Or you can just stare into the distance and see the planet beneath your feet. There aren't many big things happening here. We will travel to different continents around the world, making small stops that let you explore the different cities – a tour of all continents except the Northern Frozen, according to Charlie, because the climate is too cold and unpredictable for safe travel. After everything is done, the ship will return and land in the city of nature to say goodbye to everyone and thank them for traveling.

		- ### Post event
			- In the same event, the diverse [[Festivities]] of the capitals are promoted. Right after the travel is over, players will have the opportunity to visit the different series independently and participate in the events that take place there. These events are meant to expose people to the different cultures around the world, as well as demonstrate what they can achieve with their own creativity. I wanted to show that the world is filled with people to meet, and not just NPCs whose objective is to make you stronger.
