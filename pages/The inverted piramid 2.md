	- ## Story
		- ### Prelude
			- After natures event weird things started to happen, the world got more dangerous, monsters started appearing more, stuff got strange, the prophesy from the shooting star seemed to have some truth. But new opportunities also appear.
		- ### Main part
		-
		- A location was discovered after a fight erupted on the outskirts of the Will city. The initial clash between the two parties unburied what appeared to be a giant, black platform. Upon closer inspection, the platform elevated, revealing itself to be not just a platform, but an entire pyramid, inverted and capable of floating independently. People believe might be a human, structure that had somehow remained hidden until now. According to the laws of the Will city, anyone was permitted to attempt entry.
		-
		- The place defies all logic. Inside the pyramid, everything appears significantly larger, like a tower with platforms that become increasingly wide and intricate, filled with monsters and creatures to battle challenges, as well as treasures and historical artifacts concealed within the walls. As one ascends higher within the pyramid, the enemies become more formidable, the challenges harder and the potential rewards become more valuable.
		-
		- Once inside the pyramid, death is a real possibility due to the inherent dangers. Should you die, you will be returned to the entrance, and must ascend the tower once more. The resources you collected will remain, but you must return to your previous location. Upon reaching that point, you will encounter a deceased version of yourself, whom you must defeat to proceed.
		-
		- ### Post event
		- The event marks the discovery and initial promotion of this place, but the inverted pyramid will remain a permanent location on the map, accessible to all. The pyramid seems to constantly change, regardless of who enters, and the treasures within appear to align with the needs of the entrant. If two individuals enter, they will be placed in separate instances of the pyramid unless one grants the other access to their instance. However, during the pyramid's initial promotion, this does not occur. It is a global pyramid, a global area accessible to all, more dangerous and filled with greater treasures.

	- ## Concepts and notes
	- ### Interesting stuff to find
	- Within the global version of the inverted pyramid, upon reaching certain floors, one will encounter stories and mysteries.
		- Of particular interest is a painting, with 6 figure: two women, two men, and two figures who have been removed from the tapestry, as if someone sought to erase them.
	-
		- One may also find unnecessarily fancy toys and discover the diary of someone named Nisha, discussing her difficulties understanding her younger sister whose name is never mentioned.
		-
		- There are many discoveries to be made, but the event also specifically involves the Graveborn, as the pyramid possesses the unusual ability to resurrect the dead as distorted versions of themselves. If you die and get to the floor you lost you will find the copy as a boss to overcome.
	-
		- There are 500 floors in this structure. It is unlikely that anyone will reach the 500th floor on their first attempt, especially this early on. Perhaps during the third arc, but currently, people are not strong enough. On the final floor, you will find a bottle containing a liquid. Should you dare to drink it, you will fall into a slumber and awaken in a new location: the [[realm of the soul]].
