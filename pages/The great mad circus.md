# The great mad circus
	- [[The Court Jester]] has come, the great mad circus has been open to the public, COME!, COME! To a place where reality is a suggestion and rules do not exist! You will not leave the same.
	-
	- ## Characters
	  background-color:: purple
		- **NPC Court Jesters:** There are 3 court jesters within the circus. They have control over everything inside the circus, literally, they are charismatic hosts and they aren't neither good nor evil. They just have zero regard on safety, if is fun is fun, they forget many people are too fragile for their standar of fun. At the start of the event they pretento be normal people named Caster, Aster and Bob
		- NPC [[Mad hatter]] The masters in the differest sections of the circus, they obey the court jesters, but in each of their areas they are the leaders of the act.
		- NPC [[Pierrot]] staff from the circurs, actors, clowns acrobats, pierrots do anyhing inside the circus
