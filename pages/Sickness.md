## Curing a sickness
- Sickness due to being caused by a biological creature can be treated with healing magic but is largely case by case as one of the more broader categories. Some sicknesses can be cured by healing while others must have something be killed or removed, in addition some sickesses are not lethal and eventually subside while others will not. Though generally healing of a high rank or higher than the thing being treated can heal regardless. Medicine can also be made to specifically cure the specific sickness by a alchemist though the stronger the disease the harder it is to make its cure.
