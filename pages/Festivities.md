# The festivities
	- *Rather than a single event, the festivities are a set of multiple events that happen all around the planet, from Nature's Kingdom to Fantasy Living Wains City. It encompasses everything and showcases the different cultures of the planet and the way people live. It's more than mundane; it's a focal point, an opportunity to learn about things people already know. There will be a few competitions, fights, and challenges, but these are on a more fun, smaller scale.*
