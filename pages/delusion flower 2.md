- # Delusion flower
  
  **Rarity:** Uncommon
  
  **Description:** A small glowing blue flower, with an appearance similar to a poppy that grow sparsely in certain areas of the ruins. Is one of the few plants that are able to grow here in abundance despite of the metal contaminated ground and the abundance of dark mana. Despite their beauty and ability to bring light to this ever dark place, if often recommended to avoid places with too many of them. As their pollen is known to cause hallucinations in large quantities, leaving you vulnerable to your own delusions and the greater dangers lurking for prey among the flowers. This flower is also known to be the symbol of the ruined city.
  
  **Common uses:**
	- Placing a few inside a sealed container makes them a quite lasting light source
	- In lower quantities their pollen can be used as a painkiller and a replacement to sleeping pills.
	- A common ingredient in hallucinogens and potions and equipment with the intent of create illusions
