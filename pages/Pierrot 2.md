	  A pierrot is a creature that can appear anywhere at any time, and seeing one usually means trouble. They are not particularly aggressive and may not be bad, per se, but they seem to have a very distorted vision of what fun is. They will make pranks, tell jokes, and create spectacles with only themselves or by making duplications of themselves. You are advised not to trust what a pierrot says, and you are also advised not to follow them, leave them alone or just watch their spectacle and leave. They may not look like much, but pierrots are the kind of creatures you don't want to have a serious fight with.
	  
	  You can find them alone on your way or as part of a [[Mad hatter]] circus.
