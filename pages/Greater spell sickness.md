- Name: Greater spell sickness  
  Type: [[Curse]]
- Effects: Fatigue, inability to cast magic, greatly weakened abilities, burns
- Description: The consequences of using powerful magic on a body or conduit unable to handle its power, overloading the body with the strain of magical power. Greater spell sickness fries the bearers magical capabilities. Making them unable to cast magic and overall weakening other magical things such as abilties and certain racials. Severity of weakening depending on what fried the person.
- duration: N/A
- Healing: Healing magic of rank equal or higher to magic that caused the sickness.
