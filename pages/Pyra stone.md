	- Description: Pyra stones are dark stones that form in volcanic regions when mana sips into the volcanic rocks, making them very durable and practically fireproof. Is the most common construction material in the fire kingdom architecture, as it guarantees not matter the temperature of the person living there no home will ever burned down. Despite these attributes this material is useless for combat, as the stone is inert to magic and too heavy and brittle for armor, trying to wear this stone as armor is like wearing an armor made of concrete.
