## Curing a curse
	- Curses work differently from the other two categories. Requiring magic capable of cleansing or breaking the cure for its effects to be removed. High ranking healing magic may be able to cleanse weaker curses but usually high rank curses require cleansing magic to be broken. Healing may help with symptoms but isn't a cure. Not all curses are unnatural and if natural can be significantly harder to get rid of.
