- In my setting there are their gods but there are also Eldritch gods. They are one and the other counterparts, but the Eldritch gods represent the changes and movement and their concepts while the gods are the concept itself, the existence and the way people understand it. Neither gods and eldritch are unkillable. It's possible and it has happened. With gods, the god simply die and one new will be eventually created to replace it, leaving the world and the concept they're represented in chaos in the meantime.
-
- but killing an eldritch god is not as straightforward an eldritch god can be whatever you believe the concept is. you could be in front of the Eldritch God of fire and you might see a campfire you might see food being heated you might see anything you think fire represents and the only real way to actually understand and kill it will be if you manage to completely understand and manipulate the concept perfectly. And reaching that point, by doing that and killing an eldritch god, you end up becoming the replacement of that eldritch god. and Eldritch are hardly people. They are the pure and chaotic essence of the concept that represents. You will completely lose who you are in a shape for the total and complete control of the concept you now represent.
