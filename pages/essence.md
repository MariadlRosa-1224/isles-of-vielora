# Essence
-
- souls are made of a "substance" commonly known as essence, no one, not even the highest of deities understand what it really is or how it works, but they can use it to shape the creatures they create, their powers and even aspects of their personalities, a template that only when born is painted with the histories and experience they live. A persons soul most of the time ends up as a combination of essense and the power of the god who created their template. This create their physical and magical limits and weaknesses, and for the great mayority, this will forever remain unchanged, but there are those who through their will and ambitions, or sometimes through direct experimentation can break these limits.
-
- But this knowledge is also the origin of those who want to manipulate the soul, understand its inner working and use it to grow limitlessly stronger, not necessarily a bad thing if it wasn't so dangerous, souls are delicate, disturbing it risk to break it or even destroy it.
-
- Essence is the main component of the [[Souls]] . It is pretty much everything they are made of. The gods use essence to create the first beings of their and through their blessing The descendants of those first individual also get the soul that is linked to their god. But not even gods are sure what essence is, They know what is capable of, they know even them have souls composed by essence, but what or why, is their question.

-
- Obtaining clean and pure essence isn't easy. Gods won't give it lightly, as simply handling the substance is dangerous for anyone. So, to obtain it outside of a god, you have to collect it as a residue from the soul rituals gods perform, or through unknown minerals hidden very deep underground in the core island and the Tower. Therefore, the effects are unknown, but one important effect is that Essence can greatly increase someone's power by a fast magnitude, leveling them up regardless of XP. But at the same time it consumes and eventually destroys the soul.
-
- Pure essence will always be stronger than a soul that is sustained by both it and magic. So if the soul comes in contact with essence, the essence will start to rip it apart and consuming back the pure essence. The physical body acts some sort of shield that prevent this from happening but it's not unpenetrable. Too much essence can still damage someone's soul if, as an example, someone was pushed into a pool full of it.
-
- Although this will never be explicitly mentioned unless someone discovers it, it has a few impacts on history. As an example, in [Soul Chase]([[6_Soul-chase]]), Emerald is able to evolve so fast because she was almost drowned in essence. However, this also shows its side effects: essence can increase power very fast, but it can also directly damage or even destroy the soul if handled incorrectly.
