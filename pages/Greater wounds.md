- Name: Greater wounds 
  Type: [[Curse]] 
  
  Effects: Prevents injuries or limbs from healing partially or fully, sometimes leaving particularly noticeable marks on wound.
  
  Description: A natural curse caused by the attacks of immensely greater foes to oneself greater wounds often do not heal fully or prevent limbs from regenerating back leaving great warriors with scars and wounds regardless of their ability to Regen. Greater wounds are often stated to be the remnant magic of the foe preventing the wounds healing.
  
  duration: N/A
  
  Healing: Curse breaking/cleansing equal to rank of wound dealt.
