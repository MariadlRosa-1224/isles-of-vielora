- A The Court Jester is a creature with the appearance of a jester with a white mask and permanent smile. They can appear anywhere at any time in solo or in groups of up to three of them. There are no reports of them being openly dangerous, but that doesn't mean they aren't powerful, their pranks and espectacles, showing them capable of feats and a power capable of destroying cities with ease if they were to put in the effort.

- Encounters usually involve large-scale pranks, perhaps pranks affecting entire cities or groups of travelers, but not violent attacks. One thing is certain: don't follow them, and don't agree to attend their circus; a mad hatter circus is already a chaotic endeavor for their own amusement. The circus of a grandmaster is "an otherworldly experience where you can forget what is real and what is not."
