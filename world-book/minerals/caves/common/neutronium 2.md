# Neutronium

**Region:** Mana dense caverns

**Hardness:** 5.32

**Solubility:** insoluble

**Value:**

**Rarity:** Common-rare

**Melting point:** unknown

**Description:** Neutronium Is a highly volatile and dangerous metallic crystal, forming much like how pyrite forms. The purity of the crystal can easily be determined by how deep and full the black color of the crystal is, commonly deeper caverns producing more pure crystals. The crystals, although commonly found in their environment, are rare due to the extreme circumstance they need to form, despite this, Neutronium is commercially used in blast mining and demolition.
