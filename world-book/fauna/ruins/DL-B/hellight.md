## Hellight

**Rarity:** special

**Danger level:** 9

**description:** A kilometer tall creature of fire who disguises itself as a giant group of wild lights waiting to attract victims to burn them alive then devour them. They are extremely dangerous once they reveal their true nature, with a strength and fire power able to melt steel in seconds. They aren’t ranked higher because they are slow and lose interest once their prey escapes. You should be safe as long as you don’t get close to one.

**Abilities:**
- Able to melt steel in seconds.
- Fire manipulation
- heat manipulation
- near immunity to fire and fire magic
- shapeshifting
- steel piercing claws
  
  
  **Region:** Ruins
  
  **Tameability:** untameable
