## Looming shadow

**Rarity:** special

**Danger level:** 11

**description:**  A bird similar to that of a raven but in a Incredibly massive in size, being capable of being a whopping 20 feet tall and a 80 foot wingspan, these birds are named after the massive shadow that is formed when they fly above something while they remain hidden. Their wings are capable of creating powerful gusts of wind and tornadoes capable of destroying buildings. They are one of the strongest birds out there, often landing to eat the bodies of large fauna. They are quite rare to see through due to not many places having enough fauna dying to sustain one let alone a few.


**Abilities:**
- Cloaking
- 3x titanium durability feathers
- are capable of flapping wings to make tornados
- beak can crush solid steel
- fast and powerful
- very smart
  
  
  **Region:** forest
