## Spark baboons

**Rarity:** uncommon

**Danger level:** 6

**description:** A creature that often travels in packs of 5 - 10 and possesses incredible physical capabilities being a mutation of Apes. They possess a certain degree of intelligence making them already a significantly more dangerous creature than most added with their well rounded physical capabilities and their ability to naturally produce a mana rich jell that excretes from their body and collects in certain areas they become a formidable force and are not to be taken lightly. Those with experience dealing with these danger beasts will know that where there is one 1 there is always others close by and that there is sometimes a leader of the pack that is often significantly stronger than the others.

(They can grab the jell from the pockets on their body that will shape and solidify itself into spheres that they can throw and explode on impact)


**Abilities:**
- Moderate Level Intelligence
- Agile Movements
- Immense Strength
- Explosive Jell
- Pack Leaders (Rare, receive 2.5x the stats)
  
  **Region:** forest
  
  **Tameability:** tameable
