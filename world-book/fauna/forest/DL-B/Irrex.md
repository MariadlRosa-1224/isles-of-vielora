## Irrex

**Rarity:** special
 
**Danger level:** 8
 
**description:** Irrex's are often the sole predators of a vast area of forest that they only hunt in. This is because of their formidable skillset, Irrexes are naturally slow creatures given their large size and density of their muscles leaving them prey to numerous other dangerous beasts. But what they lack in speed they make up for in other areas, they possess the ability to produce a sticky gel like substance they use as a weapon to trap their prey while camouflaged. Because of this combination most dangerous beasts and travelers often take precautions to avoid meeting them. Those that do hunt them are normally experts in the field.
 
**Abilities:** 

- Anger Empowerment
- Camouflage
- Overwhelming Strength
- Sticky Jell
- High Durability

 
**Region:** forest
 
**Tameability:** tameable
