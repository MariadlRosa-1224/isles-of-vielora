# D - Flame create 

**Spell rank:** D

**Description:** This spell allows to create a flame on the side of a person's hand. They can manipulate at will around them. It doesn't do much damage but is a popular spell among beginner mages and adventures who explore dark places.

**cost:** 1 charge.

**limitations & side effects:**
Its range is just 5 meters away from the caster.

**cooldown:** none

**requirements:** n/a
