# Healing area

**Rune rank:** C

**Type of Rune:** Aereal -> ground

**Enchantment:** An area 5 meters around the rune will start shining with a green color once the rune is placed. Everyone on it will get their wounds healed and  minds feeling calmer. 2 turn on it will heal minor wounds and 4 turns medium wounds.

**Limitations & side effects:**
- Last a minimum of 2 turns. More turns will require the user to use mana to maintain the rune draining a medium amount of mana every extra turn.
- Staying too long can cause drowsiness.
  
  **Requirements:**
  
  None
