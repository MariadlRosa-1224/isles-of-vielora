# Souls

Lore and oc wise a soul is something of great value and importance, the very being of a person, something untouchable, damaging a soul, even more, destroying a soul is a crime only paid with death in most planets, this one is not the exception.

As long as the soul exists an oc can revive, same race or not, you exist as long as your soul exists.

Souls can only be changed by their creator, their god, and changing a soul means changing someone’s very being in any way pleased, giving or taking power from them, forever changing who they are, their memories, their feelings, everything.

Someone with complete control over their soul and the knowledge and ability to manipulate it, is an unpredictable force, respected even by the gods.

A power like that is desired by many, so illegal labs experimenting on souls trying to “liberate them” exist, experimenting on a soul is not better than destroying it. Especially if the damage ends up with a shattered soul.
