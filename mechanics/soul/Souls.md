- souls are the unintended consequense of consiousness, a window in which the universe can understand itself.  and from there can influence, that goes both ways, just living a life or even an afterlife is a new experience, a new perspective that is forever kept in the universe. and the universe "the will of creation" can influence souls.
-
- Lore and oc wise a soul is something of great value and importance, the very being of a person, something untouchable, damaging a soul, even more, destroying a soul is a crime only paid with death in most planets, Vielora is not the exception.
  
  As long as the soul exists a person can revive, same race or not, you exist as long as your soul exists.
  
  Souls can only be changed by their creator, their god, and changing a soul means changing someone’s very being in any way pleased, giving or taking power from them, forever changing who they are, their memories, their feelings, everything.
-
- But this doesn't mean gods really understand what a soul is or what it's made from. A soul is made out of [[essence]] , a substance that can be manipulated but hardly understood. Even gods, the minor ones, are made out of essence. The only exceptions are the guardians and God themselves.
  
  Someone with complete control over their soul and the knowledge and ability to manipulate it, is an unpredictable force, respected even by the gods.
  
  A power like that is desired by many, so illegal labs experimenting on souls trying to “unleash them” exist, experimenting on a soul is not better than destroying it. Especially if the damage ends up with a [shattered_soul.](shattered_souls)
-
- Experimenting on your own soul is frown upon for the risk, experimenting on another's soul, again their will, is a crime punished with torture and death, no matter where in the universe you are, and destroy a soul means no afterlife, no revival, not even by the hands of a god, only a memory, for those who once met it and the universe who grief their lost.

- ## Shape
  
  A soul when uninjured is usually composed of 2 major parts, (except for certain races) one part being labeled the bulb and the other the aura.
  
  The bulb is the core of the soul, a sort of container for the essence inside or the template which when damaged is cracked and causes essence to leak if too far broken.
  
  The other is the aura, a sort of glow or field given off by the bulb. When the bulb is damaged the aura weakens. 
  
  When fresh most look alike other than the slight differences in template based on who made it, and over time as experience is imprinted onto it it begins to grow and change. Reflecting to match the person's perception of not only others but themselves.
  
  The aura is like a transparent view of what the person views themselves as, it’s not very detailed, fuzzy unless you have a powerful soul sight, the bulb is always visible somewhere inside of the aura shaped by the gods template and growing different as the soul gets older. It doesn’t need to be humanoid nor look like the person at all but is common for people to view themselves as what they grew as.
