# Bio Format
template:: Bio format
	- ## General information
		- ### **Name :**
		  
		  _Your OC’s full name. It is not required to have a middle or last name._
		- ### **Race :**
		  
		  _Your OC’s race. \[see races section]_
		- ### **Age :**
		  
		  _Your OC’s age. Keep your race’s lifespan in mind - max age for any starter oc is 1000 years_
		-
		- ### **Country :**
		  
		  _The place where your oc lives. They can live under no particular flag. in which case just say none_
		- #
		- ### **Role:**
		  
		  _What is the job of your oc._**_Echelon level:_** _This is the reputation your oc has in their job, \[See roles section] you can only start with echelon 8, 7 or 6_
	- ## OC description
		- ### **_Physical Description:_**
		  
		  _What your OC looks like. This can include their body shape, their hair/eye/skin color, their special features (like horns for races that have them), etc._
		- ### **_Personality:_**
		  
		  _How will your oc act and how is they with others, self explanatory_
		- ### **_Condition:_**
		  
		  _It is also recommended to include their physical or mental conditions, such as their strength, endurance, motivations, determination… etc., as well as their illnesses, defects, etc. as they will be taken into account during balancing._
		  
		  _This doesn't apply to oc stats, saying your oc is in good shape doesn't mean he is stronger than their race or than others._
	- ## Capabilities
		- ### **Talents:**
		  
		  _What your OC is good at. Can be useful stuff, combat-related stuff, or just… anything, like painting for example. Depending on what it is, it might be taken into account in balancing._
		- ### **Training level**
		  
		  _As you rp, you can submit any “training” rp to be granted experience to level up, each level granting you an extra multiplier to your stats_
		-
		- ### **Abilities:**
		- _For context : “Abilities” are more oriented towards the special capabilities of your oc, you create these capabilities. They may be passive or they might require mana charges, they might boost your oc stats. You choose, but obviously they will be balanced_
		  
		  _You have 2 slots by default. each with up to 3 sub-abilities, keep in mind the more sub abilities the weaker they will be._ 
		  
		  _(keep in mind that while is you are allowed to have flavor and stories written into your abilities each ability cannot be longer than 250 words and each sub ability cannot be longer than 150 words and you must keep the abilities as easy to read as possible, reviewing overly long and complicated written abilities takes a lot of time and can lead to misunderstandings easily, so please just go straight to the point)_
		  
		  _Balancers can and will ask you to simplify your writing if they cannot understand what you are trying to say. You have been warned._
		- #### **_Stats:_**
		  
		  _This is a measure to prevent discussions and ambiguities both in rp and in balancing, for each ability (if applied) you must put the maximum multiplier or level they give/take in each stat both passively and actively, Any stat increase/decrease mentioned here must also be mentioned in the respective ability._
		  
		  _(I will put the table here)_
		- #### **_Racial traits:_**
		  
		  _There’s no need to mention the Base abilities of your race, but mention the ones you chose as you level up._**_Lore traits:_**
		  
		  _These include boons, curses, conditions, etc. your oc can get in rp via the lore, this section must remain empty unless told otherwise._
		- ### **Spells/runes/etc:**
		  
		  _These are a form of pre-existing magic you can choose from \[the magic forum] unless said otherwise everyone starts with a max of 6 D ranks and 4 c rank spells and runes. Just list the name and rank of the spells chosen_
	- ## Inventory
		- _You have 2 options, either list the items your oc has and the link to their equipment and familiars or put them in a separate document and link it to this one._
		- ### **Items:**
		  
		  _It can be both miscellaneous items or stuff in the world book, for the first there’s no a real limit as long as it makes sense but for the second you can only start with a max of 3 common and 1 uncommon item_**_Equipment:_**
		  
		  _custom magic item that must be submitted separately_
		- ### **Familiars:**
		  
		  _custom fauna or fauna from the world book you have tamed (for starters up to uncommon danger level 3) that must be submitted separately._
	- ## Backstory
		- _Your OC’s backstory. Avoid nobility/stars/gods/space/other worlds/etc related backstories unless you have explicit OOC permission._
		  
		  _(And no, you can’t create a “new/fallen country” for the sole purpose of granting yourself permission.)_
